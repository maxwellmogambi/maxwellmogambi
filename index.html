Analyzing video games
using SQL

According to Mordor Intelligence, video games are big business: the global gaming market is projected to be worth over $300 billion by 2027. With so much money at stake, major game publishers are highly incentivized to create the next big hit. But are games improving, or has the golden age of video games already passed? In this project, I analyze video game critic and user scores, as well as sales data for the top 400 video games released since 1977. I seek to uncover a potential golden age of video games by identifying release years favored by users and critics, and explore the business side of gaming through sales data analysis.
